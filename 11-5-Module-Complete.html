What you discovered
  • A little success made it impossible to keep the repair work in your head.
  • By sorting your items, you drew a line: related to the repair work, or not.
  • That line is not about where things are or what they are used for. It is about whether they are part of the repair work.
The deeper point

The first thing anyone who runs an activity has to do, from a one-person bike repair shop to a large company, is decide what belongs inside the activity and what stays outside it.

You just did that by hand, sorting a pile of items before learning the accounting language for it. Keep that line in mind: everything else builds on it.
