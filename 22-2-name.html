Name the LLC

Before you can file, the LLC needs a name.

Until now, you have called it “the repair work” or “the operation.” Now you need the name that will go on the filing. It can carry your name, but once the state accepts the filing, the LLC will be legally separate from you.

The name of your LLC will be:
[Your Name]’s Bike Repairs, LLC

Type your name to personalize the LLC name, or leave it blank to continue with the placeholder.
