What You Discovered
  • Every Thing on your list got a dollar amount, but the amounts didn’t all come from the same place: a receipt for what you bought, an eBay comp for the gear you’d owned a while, a shelf count for the supplies.
  • Maria has her own name for the list — Insurable Property: only what you own and could lose. You bought $500 of parts, but only the $200 still on the shelf goes on it.
  • You valued one item in each section by hand, then finished the rest in one pass. Same move as last time: once the pattern’s clear, the volume is easy.
The deeper point

This is the same list of Things from last time. All items are now sorted into categories with a number on every line. But it’s only one slice of it: the part an insurer cares about. Other people will look at the very same list and see something different. The bigger picture it all belongs to is still being built. It’ll have a name by the end of the module.
