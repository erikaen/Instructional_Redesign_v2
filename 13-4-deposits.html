Tutorial 1.3: Anna and the Bank Statement

Bicycle repair shop · ~20 min

Anna stopped on the eight customer deposits — money coming in from customers across the month. The bank statement showed them as money in, nothing more. But what were they for?

ANNA
“So these deposits are from cash you got from customers, but why did they give it to you? Let’s check the receipts.”
Some receipts are clearer than others. For each deposit, decide what it was — or whether you can tell at all.
