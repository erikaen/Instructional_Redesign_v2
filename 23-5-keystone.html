When Did It Become Real?

You have now seen the key pieces of the Operating Agreement: who the Sole Member is, what the Sole Member contributes, and what limited liability changes.

So what did this agreement actually do?

What did the Operating Agreement and contribution mainly do?
What the Agreement Did

The Operating Agreement did not create new values. It organized the LLC: who owns it, who can act for it, what was contributed, and what limited liability means. The existing bike repair business is now shown as contributed to the Company at its existing book amounts. From here, the LLC has a clean formal starting point for its own records going forward.
