What You Put In

You read it in your agreement: Section 9, Capital Contributions.

This section says what the Sole Member contributes to the Company. Here, it is the existing bike repair business: the assets and liabilities already recorded in the repair-work books.

The key phrase is existing book amounts. You are not starting over, and you are not making up new values. You are carrying the same recorded amounts into the LLC.

§9. “The Sole Member contributes the assets and liabilities of the bicycle-repair business, at their existing book amounts, as set forth on Schedule A.”
