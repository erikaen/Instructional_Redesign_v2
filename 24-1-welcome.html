Activity 2.4: The Entity’s Own Number

Module 2 · Getting Formal · ~10 min · Phase 1 of 5

An Identity of Its Own

The LLC exists, it has its rules, and it owns the business. But to the outside world it still has no identity of its own. You go to open a business bank account — and the bank stops you:

THE BANK
“We can’t open this in your name. We need the LLC’s number — its EIN — not your Social Security number.”
Until now, the repair work has run through your personal accounts — your personal checking (Elm City Bank, $4,074) and your personal credit card (Harborline Visa) — both co-mingled: business deposits sitting next to your paycheck, parts charges next to your groceries. You’ve sorted them apart by hand every month since 1.1. The entity needs its own.
