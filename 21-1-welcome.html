The Call from Maria

The repair work has grown. You have more gear, more customers, and more cash moving through the operation than when you started. Since the first week, your books — Bike-Repair.xlsx — have helped you keep what you do in your bike-repair role separate from the rest of your life.

Then Maria, your insurance agent, calls back with a different concern. This time, she is not asking about your tools and parts. She is asking what happens if a customer gets hurt and decides to hold you personally responsible.

What you’ll discover
  • What Maria is really warning you about, and why an insurance policy alone won’t solve it.
  • Who, besides you, actually treats your bike-repair role as separate from the rest of your life.
  • What it would take to make that separation more than something you maintain by hand.
