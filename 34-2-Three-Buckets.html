Tutorial 4: The Cash-Flow Statement

Module 3 · The Story Between the Snapshots · ~10 min · Phase 2 of 4

Three Buckets

Every dollar that moved through Cash this season is a fact — they’re all in the Cash line on the Assets tab, and they’re laid out again on a new Season Cash tab beside the ones you know. The June 1 opening deposit isn’t one of them; that happened before the season started. Sort them into three piles, by purpose: running the season day to day, buying long-lived gear, or an owner moving money. Click a row, then click the pile it belongs in.
