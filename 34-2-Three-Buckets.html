Module 3 · The Story Between the Snapshots · ~10 min · Phase 2 of 4

Three Buckets

Every dollar that moved through Cash this season is a fact — they’re all in the Cash line on the Assets tab, and they’re laid out below as one Season Cash list. The June 1 opening deposit isn’t one of them; that happened before the season started. Sort them into three piles, by purpose: running the season day to day, buying long-lived gear, or an owner moving money. Click the beside a row, then choose the pile it belongs in — then sort the rest in one go, and check your sort.
