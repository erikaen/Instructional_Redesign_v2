Two Kinds of Reason

Read any reason in the GENERATED pile and it does one of just two things: it’s money the work brought in, or it’s something the work used up. Nothing else.

Right in the workbook: click a reason in the pile, then click the pile it belongs in. Do a few and the pattern will be clear — then you can sort the rest in one go.
