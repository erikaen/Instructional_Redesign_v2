Two Kinds of Reason

First, the period line. The question is how the shop did this summer, so May’s rows stay exactly where they are — set aside, untouched. What’s left is the season — and read any season reason and it records one of just two things: the work delivered — a repair finished, a frame handed over — or something got used up delivering. Nothing else.

In the list below: click the beside a reason, then choose the kind it belongs to. Do a few and the pattern will be clear — then you can sort the rest in one go, and check your sort.
