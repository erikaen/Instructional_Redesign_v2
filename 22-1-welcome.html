Tutorial 2: Filing for a Legal Body

Module 2 · Getting Formal · ~10 min · Phase 1 of 5

Anna’s Advice

You left Maria with one thing clear: the repair work needs more than insurance. It needs a legal wall between the operation and your personal life — the kind a policy cannot create.

So you call Anna. She kept the books at City Cycle for years and helped the owner set it up as an LLC.

She doesn’t overthink it.

ANNA
Form an LLC. That’s the legal wall Maria was talking about. I helped the owner at City Cycle set one up. Back then, we used an attorney. These days, the state filing itself is pretty straightforward, though you still want it checked carefully.

Start with the filing that brings the LLC into existence. Once the state accepts it, your bike-repair role has a legal body of its own — something the bank, customers, and the law must recognize as separate from the rest of your life. You recognized the role first; now you formalize it.
What you’ll discover
  • What it takes to bring a legal entity into existence.
  • What changes the moment that legal entity exists — and what, surprisingly, does not.
  • Which came first: the legal body, or the operation it wraps.
