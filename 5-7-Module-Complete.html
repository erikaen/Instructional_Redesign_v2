The names you earned
  • You drew a boundary for a purpose. That boundary defines the entity. You made it; you did not find it.
  • Recorded Assets minus recorded Liabilities is Equities — a definitional residual, not a pile of cash and not your personal net worth.
  • Assets − Liabilities = Equities is the accounting identity. It holds by construction, and the page that lays it out is the balance sheet.
  • The residual can come from two sources: contributed and generated. A no-owner entity calls the residual Net Assets.
  • Every change has two sides. That is double-entry, and it is how every number gets onto the statements.
