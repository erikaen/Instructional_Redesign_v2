Reading the Season

On the Reasons tab, you drew two ruled, dated lines in GENERATED: June 1, when the LLC’s books opened, and August 31, today. You use those dates to mark the season you want to study.

Read between them. Every GENERATED row below the June 1 line and above the August 31 line records activity from this season. Those rows are the season’s activity in the records.
