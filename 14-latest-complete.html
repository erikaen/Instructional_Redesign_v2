What You Discovered
  • You kept a Reasons window next to every list — one row for the why behind each change.
  • Most reasons live in two windows — same event, two changes. You didn’t arrange that, and half the second rows came from records others kept: the statement, the bank.
  • The matched pairs just shuffle — have and owe move together, so your net doesn’t budge.
  • The reasons that move your net are two plain kinds: what you contributed and what the work generated.
  • And one only showed up when you tallied: the $960 of your own cash the work quietly drew down — the first real line between the repair work and you.
Coming Next

You have three things now: what you have, what you owe, and the reasons your net moved. In 1.5 you’ll lay them out on one page, watch how they fit together — and the names you’ve been circling finally arrive.
